# Insights Report – Museum Artwork Collection

## 1. How modern are the artworks?
Most artworks were created between the late 1800s and early 2000s, with a noticeable increase in modern artworks after 1950.

## 2. Which artists are featured the most?
The dataset shows that Ludwig Mies van der Rohe appears as the most featured artist, followed by several other frequently represented creators.

## 3. What types of artwork are most common?
Photographs dominate the collection, followed by prints, paintings, and sculptures.

## 4. Are there any trends in acquisition dates?
Acquisition activity increased significantly after the 1960s, showing the museum’s growing expansion efforts.

## 5. Artist diversity analysis
The dataset includes artists from multiple nationalities and genders, with a higher representation of male artists. The dashboard visualizes this distribution clearly.
